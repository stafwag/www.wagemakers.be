Lookat - Bekijk 1.4.4 (GNU/Linux, Mac OSX, *BSD, Solaris)

Lookat / bekijk is a program to view Un*x text files and man pages.

  • Download(.tar.gz)
  • A FreeBSD port is available at:
    http://www.freshports.org/sysutils/lookat/
  • Lookat is available on Fedora GNU/Linux: https://admin.fedoraproject.org/pkgdb/acls/name/lookat
  • A FreeBSD port is available at:
    http://cvsweb.openbsd.org/cgi-bin/cvsweb/ports/sysutils/lookat/
